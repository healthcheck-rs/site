Healthcheck.rs

Add uptime monitoring to your site in less than a minute.

Healthcheck.rs regularly pings your site to make sure that it is available for your users. Check your site's analysis page to see your site's uptime over the past month, as well as its p99 latency response time. If something goes wrong with your site, we'll send you an email letting you know your service is unavailable.

Quick Start

Enter your site's health check route, and we'll start monitoring its availability. We'll even give you this cool Markdown badge you can add to your Github, too!
